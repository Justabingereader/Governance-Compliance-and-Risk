# Cyberattack Contingency Plan

## Executive Summary

This project simulates the role of a newly appointed CISO at a rapidly scaling e-commerce organisation ("Smart Ltd.") responding to a multi-stage ransomware incident. The scenario mirrors realistic attack patterns: a two-month reconnaissance phase, a vishing-based social engineering compromise, privilege escalation via buffer overflow, lateral movement to a DNS server, and DNS spoofing — culminating in encryption of critical business systems including payment platforms, customer databases, and order management.

The deliverable is a comprehensive cyberattack contingency plan that covers immediate incident containment, business continuity, stakeholder communications, and long-term remediation. It also addresses the regulatory and audit obligations that a customer-data-handling retail operation must meet in the aftermath of a breach.

**Key skills demonstrated:** incident response planning, threat modelling, social engineering awareness, privilege escalation analysis, DNS attack vectors, business continuity management, regulatory compliance, and executive-level security communication.
