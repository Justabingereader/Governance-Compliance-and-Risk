# Ransomware Mitigation

## Executive Summary

This project produces a comprehensive ransomware mitigation strategy for "Televula," a 1,500-person software company whose products serve the healthcare sector — a high-value ransomware target given the sensitivity of patient data and the operational criticality of healthcare systems.

The strategy is structured across four tiers: **Strategy** (vision, prevention, backup/recovery, detection and response, ransom stance, communications, and governance); **Policy** (roles and responsibilities, regulatory compliance, risk appetite, control objectives, audit cycles, and incident escalation procedures); **Procedure** (step-by-step prevention, incident response, and compliance workflows); and **Management** (operational security, technical controls, collaboration, and a before/during/after attack playbook).

The work contextualises the threat landscape with current intelligence — including AI-accelerated attack cycles and the DaVita ransomware incident involving 2.7 million patient records — and positions the organisation's supply chain risk to healthcare clients as a core driver for investment in resilience.

**Key skills demonstrated:** ransomware threat modelling, security strategy development, policy and procedure writing, RACI matrix design, incident response planning, business continuity, regulatory compliance (healthcare sector), and risk appetite definition.
