# ISM & Compliance Report

## Executive Summary

This two-part report applies Information Security Management (ISM) frameworks to real-world organisational contexts, demonstrating the ability to translate regulatory requirements into actionable security governance.

**Part 1** maps ISO 27001 controls onto AXA, a French multinational insurance corporation operating across multiple continents. The analysis covers governance structure, leadership commitment, RACI accountability, risk assessment methodology, incident response, internal audit processes, and a justification for why ISO 27001 is the appropriate framework for AXA's risk profile and regulatory exposure.

**Part 2** conducts a GDPR compliance assessment for Dublin General Hospital, identifying crown jewel data assets, mapping data handling processes (collection, storage, access, transmission, backup, and third-party sharing) to specific GDPR articles, and producing prioritised technical and organisational recommendations. The report also outlines methods for identifying non-compliance and includes practical exercises for staff awareness.

**Key skills demonstrated:** ISO 27001 implementation, GDPR compliance analysis, information security governance, risk assessment, RACI charting, crown jewel identification, data flow mapping, and regulatory advisory writing.
